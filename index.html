About Me

I'm a Northwest native who has called Indiana home for many years now. I hold a Humanities degree in English. With a background worked in retail, editing, marketing, and customer service, I am a prime candidate for the question “what do you do with a BA in English?”

The majority of my career has been in the banking industry and I love helping people understand and improve their financial well-being.

In my free time, I enjoy solving puzzles and spending time with my family. Hobby games are a passion, as they usually combine the two things I love most!

Software development has been a natural extension of the love of puzzles. An added bonus is that I get to use my creative side!
